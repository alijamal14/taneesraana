About Dr. Tanees Raana

Leading Gynecologist in Islamabad

Dr. Tanees Raana is a highly qualified and experienced gynecologist based in Islamabad, Pakistan. With extensive training and expertise in women's health, she provides compassionate care to patients of all ages.

With years of experience in premier medical facilities, Dr. Raana has established herself as one of the most trusted gynecologists in Islamabad, specializing in both routine and complex gynecological and obstetric cases.

Qualifications:

  • MBBS
  • DGO (Diploma in Gynecology and Obstetrics)
  • MCPS
  • FCPS (Fellow of the College of Physicians and Surgeons)
  • CPSP (College of Physicians and Surgeons Pakistan)

Our Services

Gynecological Care

Comprehensive women's health services including routine checkups, management of gynecological conditions, and preventive care.

Obstetric Care

Complete pregnancy care from prenatal to postnatal, including regular checkups and delivery services.

Infertility Treatment

Specialized care for couples facing fertility challenges, including IUI and other advanced reproductive technologies.

Painless Delivery

Advanced methods for comfortable and safe childbirth experiences with state-of-the-art facilities.

Laparoscopic Surgeries

Minimally invasive gynecological surgeries including laparoscopic hysterectomy, myomectomy, and endometriosis treatment.

4D Ultrasonology

Cutting-edge 4D ultrasound services for detailed pregnancy monitoring and precise gynecological diagnostics.
